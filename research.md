## Feedback cooling atomic quantum gases

At ANU, I did research in closed-loop feedback control of quantum gases (both fermionic and bosonic), mostly with the aim of designing a new cooling technology to augment/replace evaporative cooling for ultracold atomic gases. More details to come soon!
